Products Sales Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing product sales data to understand how sales methods and customer attributes influence revenue. It applies data analysis techniques to uncover trends and provide actionable insights for business strategy.

  • Collected and cleaned sales data using Python.
  • Performed exploratory analysis to visualize monthly and regional sales performance.
  • Created bar charts, line plots, and time-series graphs to illustrate trends and patterns.

  • Email + Call sales method generated the highest and most consistent revenue.
  • Customer tenure does not significantly impact revenue, but site visits do.
  • Revenue trends remain stable over time.

  • Prioritize the Email + Call strategy to maximize revenue.
  • Focus on High-Performing Regions.
  • Monitor site visits to improve marketing efforts.
